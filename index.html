GROUPEMECA in a nutshell

GROUPEMECA is an industrial group specialized in the production of high precision springs, spring-effect parts, fine sheet metal works and induction coils. Next to the production and surface treatment of parts, GROUPEMECA accompanies its clients in the designing and prototyping of tailor-made parts.
